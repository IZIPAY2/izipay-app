How to Top Up Your Card

Follow these steps:

1
Start Top Up Click the "Top Up" button on your dashboard.
2
Select Your Card If you have multiple cards, choose the specific card you wish to fund.
3
Choose Cryptocurrency Select the cryptocurrency you want to use for the top-up.
4
Complete the Transaction Send the cryptocurrency to the wallet address displayed on the screen.
5
Confirm Payment Once the transaction is sent, press the "I have paid" button and enter your TXID (Transaction ID) to finalize the process.
Funds are credited to the card balance after 2-3 network confirmations.
